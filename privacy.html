Privacy Policy

1. Text Message Program Terms

By subscribing to Campaign text messages, you agree to receive recurring political and informational text messages (SMS/MMS), including updates, events, and fundraising requests, sent using an automated dialing system or other technology.

Message and data rates may apply.

Consent is not a condition of making a contribution or purchasing goods/services.

2. Opt-Out and Help

You may revoke your consent at any time and in any reasonable manner.

To stop receiving texts, reply STOP to any message you receive. You will receive a single confirmation message.

For assistance, reply HELP.

3. Data Collection and Sharing

The Campaign collects your mobile phone number, contact information, and any personal data you submit.

No Commercial Selling: We will not sell your personal information, including your mobile phone number, to commercial third parties.

Political Sharing: We may share your information with political organizations, candidates, or committees that share our goals, as well as with third-party vendors who provide services to the Campaign (e.g., text messaging platforms, data services).

Legal Compliance: We also disclose information as required by law, such as for campaign finance reports.
