About Heidi

A businesswoman, former CPA, and community leader with decades of experience serving Nevada.

Heidi Kasama started her professional career as a Certified Public Accountant and later expanded it as a Licensed Real Estate agent. She holds a Bachelor of Science in Accounting from Central Washington University and has extensive experience auditing major business and government institutions.

After moving to Las Vegas in 2002, Heidi opened her own real estate brokerage which grew to approximately 80 agents. She later merged with Prudential (now Berkshire Hathaway Home Services) and served as managing broker of an office of approximately 250 agents for 14 years.

Heidi served as the 2018 President for the Nevada Realtors and was honored as "Realtor of the Year" for 2018 by NVR and 2015 by LVR. She also served as President of the Las Vegas Realtors for 2014 and has been a director for the National Association of Realtors.

Elected to the Nevada State Assembly District 2 in 2020, Heidi has been a consistent conservative voice fighting for Nevada families. Now she's ready to bring that same dedication to the Clark County Commission.
